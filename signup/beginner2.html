Beginner Workshop 2 Signup

Who is this workshop for?

This workshop is for folks age 3-103 who have experience playing games to completion against family and friends.

For help understanding if this workshop is right for you or your child, please call our instructor: 619-363-2623.

Where and when do we meet?

Workshop begins the last week of November, 11/28. We meet once weekly for a total of 8 lessons, either Wed 6-7p or Sun 4-5p.

Address is 9723 Coppertop Loop NE, 98110. Enter at the SW corner of the building.

No class during winter break (Dec 19—Jan 1). Workshop ends by the first full week of February.

What happens in the workshop?

Through fun, interactive challenges, participants will explore tactical motifs, mating patterns, opening principles, chess etiquette, sportsmanship, and possibly other material suited to the skill level of participants. Thanks to small class sizes, we can tailor activities to each individual's pace of learning.

By the end of the workshop, folks will be able to play and enjoy chess at a competitive tournament level. They will have increased tactical vision and theoretical knowledge, including an appreciation for the opening, middlegame, and endgame phases.

How much does it cost?

Total cost is $195 per participant. Everyone will walk away with their very own triple-weighted chess set and travel bag.

After we receive your registration form, we will contact you to request a $45 deposit that reserves your participant seat.
